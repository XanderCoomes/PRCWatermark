From personalized learning pathways to automated grading systems, AI is redefining how knowledge is delivered, accessed, and assessed. However, while the technological capabilities of AI present exciting possibilities for enhancing educational outcomes, their integration must be approached with both intellectual rigor and ethical mindfulness.
