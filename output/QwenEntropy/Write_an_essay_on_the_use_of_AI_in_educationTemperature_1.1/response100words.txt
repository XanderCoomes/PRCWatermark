When it comes to addressing pressing global challenges, such as climate change, inequality, and resource scarcity, one often overlooked but increasingly significant force is artificial intelligence (AI). In recent years, AI has transitioned from being a futuristic concept to a practical tool with tangible impacts across various sectors—most notably, education.

The integration of Artificial Intelligence into educational systems holds immense promise for transforming how knowledge is delivered, accessed, personalized, and evaluated. By leveraging machine learning algorithms, natural language processing, computer vision, and data analytics, AI can offer adaptive learning environments that respond dynamically to individual students’ needs.
