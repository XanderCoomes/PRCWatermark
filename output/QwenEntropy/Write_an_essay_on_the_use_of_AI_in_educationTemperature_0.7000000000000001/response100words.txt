  
**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Path Toward Personalized, Accessible, and Effective Learning**

Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming various sectors of society, and one of its most promising applications lies within education. From personalized learning pathways to automated grading systems, AI technologies offer educators, students, and institutions new tools that enhance accessibility, engagement, and outcomes. As global educational challenges—such as inequitable access, diverse learner needs, and rising student expectations—grow more complex, artificial intelligence presents a transformative solution capable of adapting to individual learners while scaling efficiently across classrooms and regions.
