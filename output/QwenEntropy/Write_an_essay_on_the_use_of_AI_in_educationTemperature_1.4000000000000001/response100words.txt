  
**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Transformative Shift**

Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming numerous sectors, with one of its most promising applications being in education. From personalized learning pathways to automated grading and real-time feedback, AI has introduced a new paradigm that enhances accessibility, equity, engagement, and efficiency in teaching and learning. While concerns about privacy, ethics, and over-reliance persist, when implemented thoughtfully and inclusively, AI holds immense potential for redefining how knowledge is delivered and acquired.
