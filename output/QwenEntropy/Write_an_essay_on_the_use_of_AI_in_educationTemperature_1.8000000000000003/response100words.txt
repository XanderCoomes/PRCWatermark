 
**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Mindful Approach to Transforming Learning**

Artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force across numerous sectors, with education being one of its most promising frontiers. As digital technologies reshape traditional learning environments, AI is steadily integrating into classrooms—from personalized lesson plans to intelligent tutoring systems—to make teaching more adaptive and students' learning more effective. However, while acknowledging these innovations’ benefits, it is equally important to approach their adoption through a mindful lens that values human-centric principles, equity, emotional well-being, and sustainable growth.
