 

The Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Education: A Path Toward Personalized, Inclusive, and Effective Learning

Artificial intelligence (AI), once confined to science fiction, has emerged as a transformative force across numerous sectors—including healthcare, transportation, finance—and is now making significant strides in one of society’s most vital domains: education. From adaptive learning platforms to intelligent tutoring systems, from automated grading to personalized feedback mechanisms, AI offers powerful tools capable of enhancing both teaching practices and student outcomes.
