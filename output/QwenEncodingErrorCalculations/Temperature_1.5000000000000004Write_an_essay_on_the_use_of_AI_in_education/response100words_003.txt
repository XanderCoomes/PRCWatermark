

**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: Opportunities, Challenges, and Ethical Considerations**

Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming numerous sectors worldwide, and one of its most promising applications lies within education. From personalized learning experiences to automated grading systems, AI tools have begun redefining how knowledge is delivered, accessed, and assessed. As technology continues to evolve at a breakneck pace, it becomes increasingly critical for educators, policymakers, and stakeholders to examine both the transformative potential and the ethical challenges associated with integrating artificial intelligence into educational environments.
