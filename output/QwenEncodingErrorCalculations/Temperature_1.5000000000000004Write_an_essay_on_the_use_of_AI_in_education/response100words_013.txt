Title: The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Paradigm Shift Toward Personalized, Accessible Learning

In recent years, artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force across numerous sectors, with education being one of its most promising arenas for change. From predictive analytics to adaptive learning systems, AI is reshaping how students learn, teachers instruct, and institutions manage educational outcomes. This essay explores the evolving role of AI within formal and informal education environments—highlighting benefits such as personalized instruction, increased accessibility, administrative efficiency—and examining ethical concerns related to data privacy, equity, and algorithmic bias.
