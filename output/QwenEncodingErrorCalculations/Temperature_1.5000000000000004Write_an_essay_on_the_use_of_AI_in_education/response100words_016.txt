 

**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Mindful, Equitable, and Transformative Perspective**

Artificial intelligence (AI) is no longer a futuristic concept confined to science fiction—it has arrived at educational institutions worldwide, reshaping how students learn, teachers instruct, and administrators manage curricula. From personalized learning pathways to automated grading systems, AI promises not only efficiency but also inclusivity, scalability, and emotional insight into student engagement. However, as with any powerful technology, its integration into education must be guided by mindfulness—ethical considerations, equitable access, and respect for human agency.
