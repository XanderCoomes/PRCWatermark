The Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Education

Artificial intelligence has long been a subject of fascination for technologists, scientists, and educators alike. Over recent years, there is growing interest within educational institutions to explore how artificial intelligence can be harnessed not only as a tool but also as a transformative force reshaping pedagogy and learning experiences. From automated grading systems to personalized tutoring platforms, this new frontier offers solutions that could help make education more equitable, efficient, and accessible.
