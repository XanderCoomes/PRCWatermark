 

**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: Opportunities, Challenges, and Ethical Considerations**

From personalized learning pathways to automated grading systems, AI technologies offer unprecedented potential for enhancing teaching methodologies, improving student outcomes, and democratizing access to quality education. However, this rapid integration also brings forth complex challenges related to equity, privacy, ethical design, and pedagogical integrity.
