  
Title: The Integration of Artificial Intelligence in Education  

The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into education represents one of the most transformative developments in modern pedagogy. As technology continues to advance, educational institutions around the world have begun leveraging AI tools not only as a support mechanism but as central components of teaching and learning processes. From personalized lesson plans to adaptive assessments and intelligent tutoring systems, AI offers numerous benefits that enhance both student engagement and instructional effectiveness.
