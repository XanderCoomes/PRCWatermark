 

**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: Opportunities, Challenges, and Ethical Considerations**

Artificial intelligence (AI) is rapidly transforming various sectors, with education emerging as one of its most promising frontiers. From personalized learning pathways to automated grading systems, AI has introduced a wave of innovation aimed at enhancing teaching methodologies, improving student outcomes, and addressing systemic inequities in access to quality instruction. However, while these advancements hold immense potential, they also raise complex ethical, social, and pedagogical concerns that must be carefully navigated.

### Enhancing Personalized Learning

One of the primary advantages of integrating AI into education
