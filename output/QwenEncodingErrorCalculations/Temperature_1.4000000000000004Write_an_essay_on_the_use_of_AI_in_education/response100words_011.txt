 Writing with Purpose: The Role of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Modern Education

In recent years, the integration of artificial intelligence (AI), once relegated to science fiction, has entered mainstream discourse — particularly within the realm of education. As educational institutions worldwide face growing challenges related to student engagement, personalized learning outcomes, resource distribution, and administrative inefficiencies, a new paradigm is emerging one powered by machine learning algorithms, data analytics, natural language processing, and adaptive tutoring systems. The deployment of AI technologies promises significant improvements not only in access and efficiency but also in equity and personalization—key goals that align with modern pedagogical values.
