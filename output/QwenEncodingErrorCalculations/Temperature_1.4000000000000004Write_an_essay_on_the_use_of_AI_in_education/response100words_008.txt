It is vital that we understand, recognize, and appreciate the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in modern education—a shift so profound it may very well redefine how knowledge is created, delivered, personalized, and accessed across all learning environments.

The integration of AI into educational systems has moved from a speculative technology to practical reality with far-reaching implications for learners, teachers, curriculum developers, administrators, and policymakers alike. From intelligent tutoring platforms tailored to individual students' needs to adaptive learning programs capable of dynamically adjusting content based on performance data, AI provides unprecedented opportunities to personalize pedagogy.
