Title: The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education

Introduction  
Artificial intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing various industries, including healthcare, finance, and transportation. In recent years, its role in shaping modern society has increasingly infiltrated the realm of education. As institutions strive to meet evolving learning demands—especially amid challenges such as digital divide gaps and curriculum innovation—the integration of artificial intelligence into educational systems holds vast potential. From personalized instruction and automated assessments to smart tutoring platforms, AI promises transformative advancements that aim not only to improve efficiency but also equity within pedagogy.
