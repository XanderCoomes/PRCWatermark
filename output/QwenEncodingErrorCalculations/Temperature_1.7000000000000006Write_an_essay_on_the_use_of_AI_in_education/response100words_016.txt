Introduction

The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) into educational systems represents one of the most significant shifts witnessed across modern pedagogical landscapes. From automating routine administrative tasks to creating intelligent tutoring programs, AI technologies offer educators powerful tools that can personalize learning experiences and democratize access to quality education worldwide. As machines grow increasingly capable at interpreting data, identifying patterns, and adapting responses based on user input, they present transformative potential for improving teaching practices as well as learner outcomes.
