Title: The Transformative Role of Artificial Intelligence in Education

Introduction  
The rapid advancement of technology has sparked a paradigm shift across numerous industries, with education being one of the most transformative sectors influenced by artificial intelligence (AI). Far beyond automating repetitive tasks, AI is introducing innovative solutions that cater to personalized learning needs, streamline administrative processes, enhance teacher effectiveness, and expand access to quality education for learners worldwide. As educational institutions seek modernization and inclusivity, the integration of AI marks not just a technological evolution but a fundamental reimagining of how knowledge is acquired, delivered, and assessed.
