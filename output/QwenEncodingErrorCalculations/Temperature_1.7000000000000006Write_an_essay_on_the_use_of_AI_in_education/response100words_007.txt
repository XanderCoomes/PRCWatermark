

**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: Opportunities, Challenges, and Ethical Considerations**

Artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force across various sectors, and one of its most promising applications is within the field of education. From personalized learning pathways to automated grading systems, AI technologies offer innovative solutions that can address longstanding challenges in teaching and student engagement. However, alongside these advantages come ethical concerns, equity issues, and societal implications that must be carefully examined.
