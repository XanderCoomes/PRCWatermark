Title: The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education

Introduction  

The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into educational systems represents one of the most transformative developments in modern pedagogy. As technology advances, so too does our capacity to personalize learning experiences, automate administrative tasks, enhance assessment methods, and improve access to knowledge for learners across diverse backgrounds. From intelligent tutoring systems to natural language processing tools that assist with writing or reading comprehension, AI is reshaping how we teach and learn. While concerns about privacy, equity, and job displacement remain valid, when implemented thoughtfully, AI has immense potential to democratize quality education globally.
