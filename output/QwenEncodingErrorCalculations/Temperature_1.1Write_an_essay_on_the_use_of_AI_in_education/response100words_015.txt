  
**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Mindful Approach to Transforming Learning**

In recent years, artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force across numerous sectors, including healthcare, finance, transportation, and—increasingly—education. As classrooms around the world seek more personalized, efficient, and equitable learning experiences, AI offers unprecedented opportunities for innovation. However, with great power comes significant responsibility. The integration of AI into education must be approached not merely through technological adoption but through a mindful, ethical, and human-centered lens that prioritizes students' well-being, equity, and critical thinking.
