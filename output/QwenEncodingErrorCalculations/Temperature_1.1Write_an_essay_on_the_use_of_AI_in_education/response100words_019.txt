AI (Artificial Intelligence) has become a significant force shaping various aspects of modern life, including education. From personalized learning pathways to automated grading systems, artificial intelligence is revolutionizing how students learn and educators teach.

The integration of AI into educational settings offers numerous benefits that enhance both teaching effectiveness and student outcomes. One of its most promising applications lies in adaptive learning platforms. These tools analyze individual learners' performance data—such as quiz results, time spent studying, and areas where they struggle—and dynamically adjust content delivery accordingly.
