  
The Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Education: A Transformative Paradigm  

Artificial intelligence (AI), once a concept confined to science fiction, has rapidly evolved into a transformative force across numerous sectors—including education. As schools and universities grapple with rising student enrollments, diverse learning needs, and evolving pedagogical demands, AI offers innovative solutions that can enhance teaching quality, personalize instruction, automate administrative tasks, and foster equitable access to knowledge. The integration of AI technologies in education is not merely technological advancement—it represents a fundamental shift toward more adaptive, inclusive, and effective systems designed around learner-centered outcomes.
