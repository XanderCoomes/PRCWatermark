 title: The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education

Introduction  
Artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force across numerous sectors, with education being one of its most promising domains. From personalized learning paths to automated grading systems, AI technologies have begun reshaping how knowledge is delivered, accessed, and retained by students worldwide. As educational institutions strive for greater equity, efficiency, and adaptability, AI offers innovative solutions that can address long-standing challenges such as resource disparities, individualized instruction needs, and administrative inefficiencies.
