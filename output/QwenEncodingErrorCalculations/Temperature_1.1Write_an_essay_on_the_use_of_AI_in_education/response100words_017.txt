  
Title: The Transformative Role of Artificial Intelligence in Education

As educational institutions worldwide grapple with increasing student populations, diverse learning needs, and evolving pedagogical demands, artificial intelligence offers innovative solutions to enhance teaching effectiveness, personalize learning experiences, and promote equitable access to quality education. From adaptive tutoring systems to intelligent grading tools, AI technologies are redefining how knowledge is delivered, assessed, and retained.
