 The Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Education: Enhancing Learning, Accessibility, and Personalization

Artificial intelligence (AI), a transformative technology rooted in machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and data analytics, is increasingly being integrated into educational systems worldwide. From personalized tutoring to automated grading and intelligent content delivery, AI has begun reshaping how knowledge is accessed, taught, and learned. While its integration presents exciting opportunities for innovation and equity in education, it also raises ethical considerations that must be carefully addressed.
