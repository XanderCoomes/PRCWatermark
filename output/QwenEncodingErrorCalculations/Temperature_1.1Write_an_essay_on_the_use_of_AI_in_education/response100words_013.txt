AI: A Transformative Force in Education

In recent years, artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force reshaping various sectors across society, with one of its most promising applications being in the field of education. From personalized learning experiences to automated administrative tasks, AI technologies offer innovative solutions that enhance accessibility, efficiency, and equity in teaching and learning processes.

One of the primary advantages of integrating AI into education is its ability to provide tailored instruction based on individual student needs.
