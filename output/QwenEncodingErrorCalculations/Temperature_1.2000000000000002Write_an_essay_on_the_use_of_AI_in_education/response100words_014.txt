Title: The Transformative Role of Artificial Intelligence in Education

Introduction  
The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into educational systems has emerged as one of the most promising frontiers in modern pedagogy. As technology continues to evolve at a rapid pace, educators, policymakers, and students alike have begun exploring how AI can enhance learning experiences by personalizing instruction, improving accessibility, streamlining administrative tasks, and supporting diverse learner needs. From intelligent tutoring systems to automated grading tools, AI is reshaping not only what we teach but also how we teach it.
