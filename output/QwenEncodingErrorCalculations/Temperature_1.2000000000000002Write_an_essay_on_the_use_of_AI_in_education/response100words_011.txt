 **The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: Opportunities, Challenges, and Ethical Considerations**

Artificial intelligence (AI) has rapidly emerged as a transformative force across various sectors, with education being one of its most promising frontiers. From personalized learning pathways to automated grading systems, AI is redefining how knowledge is delivered, accessed, and assessed. As technology continues to evolve at breakneck speed, it becomes increasingly important for educators, policymakers, and stakeholders to critically evaluate both the opportunities and challenges associated with integrating artificial intelligence into educational settings.
