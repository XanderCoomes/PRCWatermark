 Title: The Transformative Role of Artificial Intelligence in Education

Introduction  

Artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as one of the most transformative technologies of our time, with far-reaching implications across numerous sectors. Among these, education stands out as a particularly promising domain for innovation. As traditional pedagogical models face challenges such as resource limitations, student diversity, teacher shortages, and varying learning paces, AI offers innovative solutions that can personalize instruction, enhance engagement, improve assessment accuracy, and democratize access to quality education. This essay explores how artificial intelligence is reshaping modern educational systems—from classroom applications to institutional management—while critically examining ethical considerations
