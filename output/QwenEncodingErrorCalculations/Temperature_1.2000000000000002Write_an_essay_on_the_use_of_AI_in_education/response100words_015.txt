AI and Education: A Mindful Exploration

In recent years, artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force across various sectors—medicine, transportation, entertainment—and one of its most promising frontiers is education. As classrooms around the world grapple with evolving learning needs, technological advancements, and diverse student populations, AI offers unprecedented opportunities to personalize instruction, streamline administrative tasks, enhance accessibility, and promote equity in access to quality educational resources.
