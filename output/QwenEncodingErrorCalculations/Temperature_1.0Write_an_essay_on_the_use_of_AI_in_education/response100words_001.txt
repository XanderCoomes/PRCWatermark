  
**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Mindful and Academic Perspective**

Artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force across numerous sectors, with education being one of its most promising domains. From personalized learning pathways to automated grading systems, AI is reshaping how knowledge is delivered, accessed, and assessed. While this technological integration brings significant benefits—enhancing accessibility, efficiency, and individualized instruction—it also raises ethical concerns that demand careful consideration from educators, policymakers, and technologists alike.
