AI is reshaping our world, from healthcare to transportation; however, its integration into education presents both remarkable opportunities and complex challenges that demand careful consideration.

The most promising application of artificial intelligence (AI) in education lies in personalized learning. Traditional classrooms often struggle with one-size-fits-all teaching methods, where students move at different paces and have diverse needs. AI-powered systems can analyze individual student performance data—such as test scores, engagement levels, and response times—to tailor content delivery accordingly.
