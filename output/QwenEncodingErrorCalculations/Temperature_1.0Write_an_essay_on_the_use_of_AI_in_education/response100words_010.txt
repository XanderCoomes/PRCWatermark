  
The Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Education: A Mindful Approach  

In recent years, artificial intelligence has transitioned from a futuristic concept to a practical tool deeply embedded across various sectors—healthcare, transportation, finance—and increasingly so within education. As classrooms adapt to rapidly evolving technologies, AI presents both transformative opportunities and ethical challenges that demand careful consideration. This essay explores how artificial intelligence is being integrated into educational systems today, its benefits for students and educators alike, while also examining critical concerns related to equity, privacy, bias, and human agency.

At the heart of AI’s role in modern pedagogy lies personalized learning.
