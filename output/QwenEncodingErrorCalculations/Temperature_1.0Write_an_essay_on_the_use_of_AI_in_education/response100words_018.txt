  
**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Mindful, Ethical Perspective**

In recent years, artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force across numerous sectors, including healthcare, finance, transportation, and—more recently—education. As schools and universities grapple with challenges such as increasing student diversity, rising costs of learning materials, unequal access to quality instruction, and evolving learner needs, AI offers promising tools that can personalize, streamline, and enhance educational experiences.
