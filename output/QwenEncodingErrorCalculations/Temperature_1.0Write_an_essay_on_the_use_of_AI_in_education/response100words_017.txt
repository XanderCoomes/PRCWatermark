  
**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Mindful Approach to Transformation**

In recent years, artificial intelligence (AI) has emerged as a transformative force across numerous sectors—healthcare, finance, transportation—and is increasingly making its way into educational environments worldwide. From personalized learning platforms to automated grading systems, AI technologies promise to enhance teaching methodologies, improve student outcomes, and democratize access to quality education. However, while these innovations hold immense potential, their integration must be approached with mindfulness—not only regarding technological efficacy but also concerning ethical considerations, equity, emotional well-being, and human agency.
