The Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Education: A Transformative Force for Learning

Artificial intelligence has emerged as one of the most revolutionary technologies shaping modern society, with its applications extending into nearly every sector—including education. As traditional classrooms face increasing demands to deliver personalized learning experiences at scale, AI offers a powerful solution by enabling adaptive instruction, real-time feedback, automated assessment, and data-driven decision-making. This essay explores how artificial intelligence is transforming educational practices globally, examining both its potential benefits and ethical challenges.
