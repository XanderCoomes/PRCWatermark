   
  
**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: Enhancing Learning, Accessibility, and Equity**

Artificial intelligence (AI) is transforming nearly every sector of modern life, and education stands as one of its most promising frontiers. From personalized learning pathways to adaptive assessments, from virtual tutors to resource democratization, AI technologies are redefining how students learn, teachers instruct, and institutions manage educational processes. While challenges remain—such as privacy concerns, bias risks, and equitable access—the potential benefits of integrating AI into education offer a revolutionary leap forward in delivering more accessible, effective, and inclusive learning experiences.
