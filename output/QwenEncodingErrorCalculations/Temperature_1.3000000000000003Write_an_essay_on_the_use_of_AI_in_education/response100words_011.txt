  
**The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Mindful Approach**

In recent years, artificial intelligence (AI) has transitioned from science fiction to a tangible force shaping various sectors—healthcare, finance, transportation—and now increasingly, **education**. The integration of AI into educational environments presents both profound opportunities and complex challenges. As educators, policymakers, and technologists seek to modernize learning experiences for diverse populations, it is crucial not only to harness the power of technology but also to do so with mindfulness—the deliberate awareness of ethical implications, equity concerns, psychological well-being, and pedagogical effectiveness.
