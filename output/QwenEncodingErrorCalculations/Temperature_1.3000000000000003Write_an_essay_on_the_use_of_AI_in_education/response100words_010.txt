 The Use of Artificial Intelligence in Education: A Mindful, Balanced Perspective

The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into educational systems represents one of the most transformative developments of our time. As technology continues to advance at a rapid pace, schools and universities around the world are exploring ways to harness AI tools—such as adaptive learning platforms, automated grading, personalized tutoring systems, and intelligent chatbots—to improve student outcomes and streamline administrative tasks. While these innovations offer promising potential for enhancing access, equity, efficiency, and personalization within education, they also raise profound ethical, psychological, and pedagogical concerns that demand careful consideration.
