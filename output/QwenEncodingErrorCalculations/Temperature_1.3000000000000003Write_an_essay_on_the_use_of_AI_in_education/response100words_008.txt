From personalized learning paths to automated grading systems, AI tools are redefining how knowledge is delivered, accessed, and retained by students worldwide. While these advancements promise increased efficiency and equity in educational outcomes, they also raise profound ethical, pedagogical, and psychological questions that demand careful consideration.
