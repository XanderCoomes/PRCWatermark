   
The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) into education has opened up new possibilities for enhancing learning outcomes, personalizing instruction, automating administrative tasks, and fostering equitable access to quality educational resources. As technology advances at a rapid pace, educators, policymakers, and stakeholders across the globe have increasingly recognized that AI can play a transformative role in reimagining how knowledge is delivered and acquired.

One of the most significant advantages of incorporating AI in classrooms lies in its ability to provide personalized learning experiences tailored to individual students’ needs.
