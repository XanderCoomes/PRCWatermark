  
The use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Education: A Path Toward Personalized, Equitable, and Future-Ready Learning  

Artificial intelligence has emerged as a transformative force across numerous sectors, and one of its most promising applications is within the realm of education. As global challenges such as increasing student diversity, rising demand for personalized learning experiences, growing educational inequalities, and evolving workforce needs become more pressing, artificial intelligence offers innovative solutions to reshape how knowledge is delivered, accessed, and understood. From adaptive learning platforms to automated grading systems, from virtual tutors to predictive analytics, AI brings both opportunities and ethical considerations into classrooms around the world
