 Teaching at this level may mean receiving instruction from both a human teacher or even from a machine, such as artificial intelligence (AI). Recently, there has been growing attention to how we can harness powerful technologies like artificial intelligence for better teaching methods and learning experiences across all educational stages. The integration of AI into classrooms—whether it's automated grading systems providing immediate feedback on student essays or personalized tutoring platforms offering adaptive exercises based on individual strengths and weaknesses—is transforming traditional pedagogy.

One major advantage of using AI in classrooms is personalization. Every learner processes information differently; some students grasp concepts quickly while others require more time and repetition before fully understanding them
